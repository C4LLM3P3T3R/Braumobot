About

Braumobot is a discord bot created for the community!
Braumobot is here to help you with your League of Legends knowlege!
Braumobot sends request to Riot's servers to get you mastery, your account information like
rank, league points, your profile name and icon and even your last match
he will also get you through every region of Runeterra starting from freezing cold Freljord,
going through brutal Noxus and finishing on balanced Ionia.
